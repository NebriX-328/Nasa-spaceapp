Discover Exoplanets with NebriX

NebriX uses NASA open data and AI to classify signals as Confirmed, Candidate or False Positive. Upload datasets, visualize light curves, and explore discoveries.

Get Started Why NebriX?

How it works

  1. Upload CSV or JSON from Kepler/TESS/K2
  2. Analyze with hybrid AI (tabular + light-curve model)
  3. Explore results, charts and export
Demo mode: files processed locally; connect to the backend to enable real model runs.

Key features

Intuitive Upload

Drag & drop or browse for CSV/JSON files. Quick preview before analysis.

Expert Visuals

Interactive folded light curves and scatter plots to inspect each candidate.

Share & Export

Download CSV of predictions and export charts for reports or presentations.
